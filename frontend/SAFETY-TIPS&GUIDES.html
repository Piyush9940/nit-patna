Safety Tips & Guides

Your safety on the road is our top priority.

Essential Road Safety Tips

Always Wear Seatbelts

Ensure everyone in the vehicle wears a seatbelt at all times, even in the back seat.

Avoid Mobile Usage

Using a phone while driving diverts your attention and can lead to accidents.

Follow Road Signs

Obey all traffic rules and road signs to prevent mishaps and ensure smoother travel.

Control Your Speed

Drive within the speed limit. Speeding is a major cause of serious accidents.

Stay Alert at Night

Fatigue increases during night drives. Make sure you're well-rested before hitting the road.

Wear Helmets

Always wear helmets when riding two-wheelers. It saves lives and is legally required.
