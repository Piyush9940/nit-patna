Why Sleep Detection Matters?

Drowsy driving is as dangerous as drunk driving. This system helps detect signs of sleepiness and alerts you immediately to avoid accidents.

How It Works?

We use camera input, facial recognition, and AI algorithms to monitor blink rate, head nods, and other indicators to detect sleepiness in real time.

Rest Tips for Long Drives

Take a break every 2 hours

Step out, stretch, and get fresh air to stay alert.

Avoid heavy meals before driving

Light meals help avoid fatigue and drowsiness.

Play music or talk

Stimulating conversation or music keeps your brain active.
